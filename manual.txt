1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The AMK Smart Pump and Compressor Control System v2.1 is an industrial-grade automation solution built on the ESP32-S3 dual-core architecture. It uses FreeRTOS to separate motor safety control from network services, ensuring the pump stays safe even if the internet is slow.

2. OPERATIONAL MODES
- Water Pump Mode: Standard level-based control (starts at Low %, stops at Full %).
- Air Compressor Mode: Pressure management using a solenoid unloader sequence (Pin 10) for head-pressure venting.
- Master/Slave Mode: Master (Sump) monitors bottom water. Slave (Roof) monitors top tank and follows Master's safety state.

3. HARDWARE SPECIFICATIONS
- Motor Control: Pin 8 (Relay)
- Solenoid Valve: Pin 10 (Venting)
- Flow Sensor: Pin 18 (Dry-run monitoring)
- Ultrasonic: Pins 5 & 6 (Tank depth 1ft to 7ft)
- Voltage: Pin 4 (ZMPT101B RMS monitor)
- RGB LED: Pin 48 (Visual Status)
- LCD: 20x4 I2C (Status Display)

4. PROTECTION MECHANISMS
- Dry-Run Guard: Stops motor if no flow is detected on Pin 18 after 30 seconds.
- Auto-Retry: System rests for 30/60 minutes after a Dry-Run lock before trying again.
- Thermal Cool-Down: Mandatory rest period after 1 hour of continuous running.
- Voltage Guard: High/Low cutoff (Default 170V-250V) with median sampling to prevent flickering.
- DND Mode: Smart scheduling prevents auto-start during night hours.

5. PHYSICAL USER INTERFACE
- Single Click Button: Toggle Pumping / Reset Alarm.
- Double Click Button: Show IP Address, Device ID, and License on LCD.
- Long Press (3s): Trigger WiFi Configuration (AP Mode).
- NeoPixel Colors: Yellow (Pumping), Blue (Cooling), Purple (Settling), Red Flash (Error), Green (Online).

6. ဖြစ်ရပ်များအား ဖြေရှင်းနည်း (Myanmar Troubleshooting)
- SNR ERR (ဆင်ဆာအမှား): Ultrasonic sensor ကြိုးလွတ်နေခြင်း သို့မဟုတ် ရေငွေ့ရိုက်နေခြင်း စစ်ဆေးပါ။
- DRY ALRM (ရေမတက်): ရေစုပ်ပိုက် လေခိုနေခြင်း သို့မဟုတ် အောက်တိုင်ကီ ရေကုန်နေခြင်းကို စစ်ဆေးပါ။
- OVER/UNDER V (ဗို့အား): မီးအားမမှန်ပါ။ စက်မှ အလိုအလျောက် ပိတ်ပေးထားခြင်း ဖြစ်သည်။
- MANUAL RESET: Manual Button (Pin 9) ကို တစ်ချက်နှိပ်၍ Alarm ပိတ်နိုင်ပါသည်။
